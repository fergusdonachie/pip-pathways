## Introduction


### Digital Interventions

SilverCloud is a digital platform that provides online CBT (Cognitive Behavioral Therapy) programs to support mental health and well-being. For individuals with Multiple Sclerosis (MS), SilverCloud offers a specific program designed to help manage symptoms and improve overall well-being. This program helps users understand the connection between their physical and mental health, and learn techniques to cope with low mood, anxiety, and stress related to MS.


#### How to refer

Refer via SCI Gateway...Mountainhall...Clinical Psychology...Psychology and choose Digital Interventions
