### Description


### Who is it for?


### Format


### Additional comments

- 2 weekly appointments
- Food reintroduction for 12 weeks
- Weight Maintenance for 18 months

BMI >27kg/m2Type 2 diabetes <6 years

- Virtual Groups
- Dietitian

Aims to achieve remission of type 2 diabetes

### Who is it for?


### Format


### Additional comments

Triaged via NHS Ayrshire and Arran Bariatric Surgery multidisciplinary team (Surgeon, specialist dietitian and clinical psychologist)Pre surgery sessionsLocal follow up for 2 years post op

- Aged 18-44 years

- BMI: 35-50kg/m2
