# Ovarian cysts - complex

Source: https://www.rightdecisions.scot.nhs.uk/dgrefhelp-nhs-dumfries-galloway/womens-health/ovarian-pathology-in-adults/ovarian-cysts-complex/
Fetched: 2026-02-23

- Normal finding - no follow up required

- Refer to gynaecology with CA125

- Follow up with TV scan US in 12 weeks to demonstrate resolution
- Refer if an increase in size
- If no change or decrease, discharge
- Note: see below re post-menopausal

- Gynaecology referral and CA125

- Gynaecology referral with CA125 (gynaecology team will decide if MRI appropriate)

- Gynae referral, CA125 and staging CT (chest, abdominal, pelvis)
- Note: If <25 yrs further tumour markers and MRI prior to CT may be arranged at the discretion of gynaecology

- Gynaecology referral, CA125 and staging CT (chest, abdominal, pelvis) but if age <40 yrs, further tumour markers and MRI - additional CT at discretion of gynaecology MDT
